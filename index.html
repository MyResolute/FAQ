My Resolute FAQs

1. What is My Resolute?

My Resolute is a comprehensive employee engagement platform designed to unify all companies within the group and enhance communication across the organization.

2. Who can use My Resolute?

The My Resolute platform is intended for all employees and HR personnel within the organization.

3. Can I access My Resolute on my mobile device?

Yes, My Resolute is accessible on mobile devices. Simply visit the MyResolute link through your mobile browser to access the platform.

4. How do I create an account on My Resolute?

Users can create their own accounts by signing up with their company email address. The email domain is restricted to the group of companies within the organization to ensure secure access. During the signup process, you will be prompted to set a password, which will be used to log in to the platform.

5. What should I do if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Follow the instructions to reset your password via email.

6. Where can I update my profile on My Resolute?

To update your personal information, navigate to the "Profile" section, and click on "Profile." From here, you can update your contact details, date of birth (DOB), date of joining (DOJ), and upload a profile picture.

7. Is it mandatory to enter both DOB & DOJ to update my profile?

Yes, both your date of birth (DOB) and date of joining (DOJ) are required to update your profile.

8. Can I edit or delete my posts?

Yes, you can edit or delete your posts. Simply navigate to the post you wish to modify and select the "Edit" or "Delete" options.

12. What should I do if I encounter technical issues?

If you experience any technical issues, please reach out to your respective HR department for assistance and support.
